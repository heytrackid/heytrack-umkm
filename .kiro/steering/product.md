# HeyTrack Product Overview

HeyTrack is a comprehensive culinary business management system for food businesses to optimize operations, maximize profits, and streamline workflows.

## Core Pillars
1. **Cost Management**: HPP (Cost of Goods Sold) calculator, recipe costing, pricing optimization
2. **Operations**: Order management, inventory tracking, production planning, supplier management
3. **Analytics**: Financial reports, profit analysis, sales trends, business intelligence

## Key Features
- **HPP Calculator**: Advanced cost calculation with scenario planning and AI-powered pricing recommendations
- **Order Management**: Multi-step order creation with WhatsApp integration and real-time tracking
- **Recipe Management**: AI-powered recipe generation with automatic cost analysis
- **Inventory Management**: Real-time stock monitoring with automated reorder alerts
- **Production Planning**: Batch tracking, quality control, and yield monitoring
- **Cash Flow**: Transaction tracking with category-based budgeting
- **Reports**: Comprehensive profit/loss, sales, and inventory analytics
- **Customer & Supplier Management**: CRM with performance tracking and analytics

## Target Users
Food businesses including restaurants, catering services, bakeries, and food manufacturers seeking to optimize costs and improve profitability through data-driven decision making.
